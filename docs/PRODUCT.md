# Career Copilot

## Mission

Help professionals make better career decisions using AI.

Career Copilot analyzes job opportunities, compares them with a candidate's profile, and provides actionable recommendations before they invest time applying.

---

# Target User (Private Beta)

Technology professionals with 3–15 years of experience looking for international or remote opportunities.

Examples:

- Solutions Engineer
- Product Operations
- Technical Customer Success
- Technical Support Engineer
- QA Engineer
- Product Manager
- Technical Account Manager
- Implementation Engineer

---

# Problem

Today's job search is inefficient.

Professionals spend hours:

- Searching job boards
- Reading job descriptions
- Tailoring resumes
- Applying

without knowing whether they actually have a strong chance of getting interviewed.

---

# Value Proposition

Upload your resume.

Paste a job link.

Receive an AI-powered analysis explaining:

- Overall compatibility
- Strengths
- Missing skills
- Resume improvements
- Whether you should apply

---

# Private Beta Goal

Validate the product with five real users.

Success means users answer:

> "This helped me decide whether I should apply."
